The History of Bago City College

The late Mayor Manuel Y. Torres envisioned giving the youth of Bago City an opportunity for tertiary education and in 1980, Bago City College Technical School was established which later became what is now Bago City College. From a Technical-Vocational School which catered to 145 students today thousands of students from Bago City, neighboring communities, and other localities have availed of affordable quality education Bago City College is known for.

Singson, Deborah Natalia, PhD
College President

Bago City College Goal

Bago City College deems it imperative to provide affordable, quality tertiary and technical education to Bago City residents and its immediate environs at par with both public and private academic institutions.

1. To create positive atmosphere for the enhancement of individuals full potentials and holistic development, so they become responsible and values-oriented citizenry. 2. To provide a strong training ground in various academic and technical courses.
3. To train and produce graduates who can provide skills and expertise in their respective fields and part of a competent pool of professionals.
